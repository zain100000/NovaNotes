Privacy Policy for Nova Notes

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

Thank you for choosing Nova Notes. Your data security and absolute privacy are the foundational core of our application.

Information We Collect

  • Zero Personal Data Collection: Nova Notes does not collect, harvest, request, or transmit any personal identifying information.
  • Complete Offline Architecture: The application is designed to operate 100% offline, guaranteeing your data stays under your physical control.
  • Local-First Storage Only: All notebooks, text elements, and tags are stored exclusively within your device's secured local database.

How Your Information is Used

Your notes exist solely for your own personal productivity and research within the app. We have no infrastructure to access, view, process, analyze, or share your content in any form.

Third-Party Services

Nova Notes operates independently of external services. The platform contains zero third-party telemetry, usage analytics trackers, ad networks, or external cloud reporting dependencies.

Children’s Privacy

Nova Notes provides a safe, clean environment for professionals and students of all age groups. Because no data collection pipelines exist within the system, the application is inherently compliant with COPPA and global child privacy regulations.

Data Security & Best Practices

Because your knowledge library never leaves your hardware, you maintain ultimate custody over your files. We strongly suggest leveraging native device security layouts—such as passcode layers, fingerprint matching, or facial recognition—to guard your local storage environment from unauthorized physical access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally refine this policy statement to mirror future local architecture updates. Any adjustments will be updated directly on this page with a revised effective timestamp.
